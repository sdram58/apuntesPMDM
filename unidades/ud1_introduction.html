1.- Introduction

In 25 years the world has changed like never before, now the world is mobile. Those of us who have a telephone from the beginning have gone from having an analog ETACS mobile of the MoviLine type to modern 5G digital terminals, now anyone speaks and navigates from anywhere. Therefore those under 20 are called digital natives. Not surprisingly, studies indicate that the mobile phone surpassed the desktop PC as the main device for accessing the Internet in Spain already in 2017.

Within the mobile world, we basically have the iOS platforms of Apple, BlackBerry and Android (after the failure of Windows Mobile / Phone from MicroSoft, and Symbian from Nokia).
